  • 20-20-20 rule — every 20 min, look at something 20 ft (6 m) away for 20 sec. Mini breaks already do this.
  • Blink consciously — screens cut your blink rate by ~60%, which is the #1 cause of dry, gritty eyes.
  • Distance — keep the monitor an arm's length away (50–70 cm).
  • Height — top of the screen at or just below eye level. Looking slightly down is natural and reduces neck strain.
  • Brightness — match your screen brightness to the room. A bright screen in a dark room burns your eyes.
  • Text size — bump it up if you're squinting. Squinting = eye fatigue.
  • Glare — no light sources directly behind or in front of the monitor. Use a matte screen or anti-glare filter.
  • Hydrate — sip water through the day. Dry body, dry eyes.
  • Warm display at night — enable Night Light / f.lux / Brave's night mode after sunset.
  • Get outside daily — natural light + far focus protects against myopia progression. Even 20 min helps.
  • Annual eye exam — wrong prescription = constant strain. Worth checking yearly if you stare at screens 8+ hrs/day.
